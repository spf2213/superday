Accounting Walk me through the three financial statements. ‚åÑ
IS: revenue ‚àí expenses = net income. BS: assets = liabilities + equity, snapshot in time. CFS: reconciles net income to cash via operating, investing, financing activities. Net income flows into retained earnings on BS.
Tip: Draw the linkages ‚Äî examiners love candidates who can sketch this cold.
Accounting How does a $10 D&A increase flow through all 3 statements? ‚åÑ
IS: pre-tax income ‚Üì$10, tax ‚Üì$3.50, net income ‚Üì$6.50. CFS: add back D&A (+$10), so OCF ‚Üë$3.50. BS: PP&E ‚Üì$10; cash ‚Üë$3.50; retained earnings ‚Üì$6.50 ‚Äî still balances.
Tip: Memorise this cold ‚Äî it's the single most tested question in IB technicals.
Valuation Walk me through the three valuation methodologies. ‚åÑ
(1) DCF ‚Äî intrinsic value, discount projected FCF at WACC. (2) Comparable companies ‚Äî EV multiples of similar public companies. (3) Precedent transactions ‚Äî acquisition multiples from prior M&A deals. Comps and precedents are relative; DCF is absolute.
Tip: Always know which gives a higher/lower value and why ‚Äî follow-ups are guaranteed.
